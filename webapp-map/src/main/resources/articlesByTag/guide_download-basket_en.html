Data cropping

Add a layer you wish to download, on the map. Choose the features you wish to download by cropping them on the map. You may select an entire dataset or crop only the features that interest you. Choose a cropping option. Cropping selects all features that intersect with the cropping data. You may choose areas with different cropping options, if you press "Move to download basket" -button in between.

An example.

Select municipality borders as the croping option, and municipality borders will appear on the map. Select the municipalities on the map, you wish to download data from. The selected areas will turn orange in color. You may deselect the areas by clicking them again, or pressing ”Empty”, which will deselect everything.

You may also crop data manually by using the rectangle. Keep left mouse button pressed and drag an area on the map you wish to download data from.

Datasets to the download basket

When you are done selecting and cropping datasets, you may move them to the download basket. Press ”Move to download basket” –button and the datasets will appear on the next tab.

The service will inform you the amount of layers and cropping options you are about to download data from. For example: Areas selected: 3pcs, do you wish to move them to the download basket?

Download basket

The datasets you have moved to the download basket, will appear on the download basket -tab. In this tab, you may review all the datasets you are about to download and their respective licenses.

Each downloadable dataset is its own entity. In case you used multiple different cropping options, each of them will be downloaded as their own respective entities. During this phase, you may still remove some datasets, you choose not to download, or return to select additional datasets. When you are done reviewing the contents of your basket, press ”Next”.

Sending the data

Enter your e-mail, when requested. The downloadable content will be sent to you via e-mail. A download link will be sent to you. If the selected datasets are very large in file size, the processing may take some time. The download link is valid for 10 days.

Your e-mail address will be used only for downloading purposes, we will not save them.

Caution!

If the download basket is closed before downloading the datasets, the cropping tool may be left active. You may close the cropping tool by selecting ”Refresh” in your browser.
